Our Story

Founded in 2010, Savory Bites brings authentic Filipino flavors to Bohol. Our founder, Chef Maria Reyes, wanted to share the traditional recipes passed down through generations of her family.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting Boholano farmers and fishermen. Our meats come from trusted suppliers who practice ethical farming.

At Savory Bites, we believe in "Lutong Bahay" (home-cooked) quality - food prepared with care, served with warmth, and enjoyed with family.
